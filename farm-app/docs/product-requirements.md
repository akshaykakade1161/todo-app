# Product Requirements

## 1. Introduction
- **Purpose**: This document captures the feature set and high‑level goals for the KrushiAbhiyanta platform.
- **Audience**: Product & engineering teams, designers, and stakeholders.

## 2. Product Vision
- Enable farmers to access tailored agricultural engineering knowledge, irrigation recommendations, machinery guidance, and government schemes through one web application.

## 3. Key Value Propositions
- Data‑driven irrigation planning.
- Curated, agronomist‑approved content.
- Seamless access to subsidy information.
- Community support and local language compatibility.

## 4. Success Metrics
- 10k active users in first 12 months.
- 20% reduction in water usage for users.
- 15% increase in subsidy claim rate.
